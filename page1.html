Who am I?

I'm Jenelle Capati, or Nelipot online. Currently a SHS HumSS student in the Philippines.

My editorial cartooning entry for SANDUGO 2020

I drew comics since I was a tiny, wee lass.

Taking inspiration from various cartoons I've watched over the course of my childhood: Spongebob, Phineas and Ferb, and Gravity Falls. I grew inspired to hone my drawing skills.

My DSPC 2019 layout entry

I did some journalism here and there.

I was initially introduced to it because of editorial cartooning. But after a long while of joining these competitions, I found solace in column writing and feature writing. But eventually, I fell in love with newspaper layouts and typography, and promptly learned it with digital programs like Photoshop and Indesign. 

My fanart of Dream, a famous youtuber

To this day, I'm honing my curiosities.

I took a break from the journalism stuff, and improved my digital drawing skills once I got hold of my own tablet. Even after all this time, I'm in love with everything about design: illustrations, typography, as well as posters. 

I can totally imagine myself creating and designing for the rest of my life.
